How do I place an order?

Ordering from GreenShop is easy! Simply browse through our wide selection of plants, add your favorites to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Follow the prompts to enter your shipping and payment information, and voila! Your order will be on its way to you.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. Rest assured, your payment information is securely processed to ensure a smooth and safe transaction.
